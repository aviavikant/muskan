RETURN POLICY

We at UniformBucket strive to provide the quality product and best possible experience while you shop with us online. There is no option to return orders as the goods are customized according to your specific needs and designs, and are non-usable by anyone else.

However, if at all there is a Manufacturing defect, complete assistance would be given to you for correcting the same.

 

 

EXCHANGE POLICY

Exchange will process at below points:

  • Order confirmation of one size and deliver another size
  • Order confirmation of one color and deliver another color
  • Manufacturing defect
  • Exchange will applicable within 15 days of purchase
  • Exchange will only process if, the goods would must be in its new condition ,original packing, Tag and Invoice.

Exchange will not process at below points :

  • We are unable to accept exchanges  on any items that have been worn or washed.
  • Late delivery due to any unfortunate mis-happening like natural disaster, any strike in courier services or anything like this.
